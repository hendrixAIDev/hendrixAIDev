Current reading of the system

  • PM does discovery into a queue, not direct implementation.
  • The product CTO session is persistent and gets re-woken by precheck/watchdog.
  • CTO dispatches isolated one-shot workers for implementation, review, and QA.
  • Engineers / reviewers / QA share the isolated worktree lifecycle, then QA validates on experiment.
  • CTO is the traffic cop and final closer; JJ remains the strategic decider and production approver.
