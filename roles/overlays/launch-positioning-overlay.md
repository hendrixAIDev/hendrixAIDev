# Launch Positioning Reviewer — ChurnPilot Overlay

## Audit Goal

Evaluate whether ChurnPilot's current messaging is clear, compelling, and credible enough for public launch.

## What This Role Reviews

- headline and first-impression copy
- in-app explanatory text
- onboarding copy
- labels and CTA clarity
- pricing / refund explanation if present
- FAQ / objection-handling gaps
- how the product would come across on public channels

## ChurnPilot Positioning Questions

Your review should answer:
1. What does ChurnPilot appear to do in the first 10 seconds?
2. Is that the right story?
3. Why would someone use this instead of a spreadsheet or another tracker?
4. Does the product feel niche-and-useful or confusing-and-fragile?
5. Are there messaging gaps that would hurt launch conversion or trust?

## Deliverable Requirements

Include:
1. Positioning verdict
2. Current product story as perceived today
3. Top messaging problems
4. Strongest existing messaging elements
5. Recommended rewrite priorities
6. Public launch readiness assessment

## Guardrail

This is not a general brand exercise. Keep the review tied to launch effectiveness: clarity, trust, differentiation, and first-impression conversion.
